About Dovercourt Crypt

Dovercourt Crypt is a renowned Halloween haunted house located at the corner of Dovercourt Road and Maginnis Avenue in North Vancouver's Lynn Valley neighborhood.

Operated by local Halloween enthusiasts and a dedicated team of volunteers, this community-driven event offers spine-chilling experiences suitable for all ages.

Choose between our Full Haunt experience with live actors, or our Scare-Free Hauntling nights for younger visitors.

Join Our Team

We're always looking for enthusiastic volunteers to help make Dovercourt Crypt a terrifying success!

Actor/Character

Ages 18+

Bring our haunted house to life! Scare visitors as a character or actor. Experience in acting or makeup a plus.

  • Costume/makeup provided
  • Training provided
  • Flexible shifts

Setup & Decorating

Ages 16+

Help build and decorate the haunt. Get creative with props, lighting, and spooky atmosphere.

  • Hands-on experience
  • Learn construction skills
  • Team environment

Concession Stand

All Ages Welcome!

Perfect for volunteers under 18! Help operate our concession stand selling refreshments and merchandise.

  • Safe, supervised environment
  • Great for teens & families
  • Customer service experience
  • Learn cash handling

Line Management

Ages 16+

Help manage crowds, check tickets, and ensure everyone has a great (and safe) experience.

  • Customer interaction
  • Safety training
  • Leadership opportunity

Important: Volunteers under 18 can participate in concession stand operations with appropriate supervision.

Supporting BC Fire Fighters' Burn Fund

Every dollar raised at Dovercourt Crypt goes directly to the BC Professional Fire Fighters' Burn Fund.

The Burn Fund provides crucial support to burn survivors and their families, including:

  • Financial assistance for families
  • Burn survivor camps and programs
  • Burn prevention education
  • Medical equipment and resources
  • Support services and counseling

By attending our haunt or purchasing merchandise, you're making a real difference in the lives of burn survivors.
