# Dovercourt Crypt - Image Download Guide

## Priority Images to Download

### From North Shore News Article
**URL**: https://www.nsnews.com/local-news/inside-the-north-shores-most-horrible-haunt-dovercourt-crypt-11366670

Look for and download these images (right-click > Save Image As):
1. **Hero/Featured Image** - Usually the main large image at top of article
   - Suggested filename: `01-hero-main-image.jpg`
   
2. **Behind-the-Scenes Setup** - Photos of building/decoration process
   - Suggested filename: `02-behind-scenes-setup.jpg`
   
3. **Interior Haunt Photos** - Inside views of the crypt/haunted house
   - Suggested filename: `03-interior-haunt.jpg`
   
4. **Actor/Character Photos** - Scary characters/volunteers
   - Suggested filename: `04-actors-characters.jpg`
   
5. **Exterior/Entrance Photos** - Outside of the haunt setup
   - Suggested filename: `05-exterior-entrance.jpg`
   
6. **Decoration Details** - Close-ups of props/decorations
   - Suggested filename: `06-decoration-details.jpg`
   
7. **Group/Volunteer Photos** - People involved in the event
   - Suggested filename: `07-volunteers-group.jpg`

## Suggested Naming Convention
Format: `XX-description.jpg` or `XX-description.png`
- XX = sequential number (01, 02, 03...)
- description = brief descriptive name (hero, interior, actors, etc.)
- Keep original extensions (.jpg, .png, .webp)
